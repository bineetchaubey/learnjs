LUCID DREAM STUDIO

  • ABOUT US

    The new immersive technologies like VR|AR|MR are redefining the way we interact with the digital world, the way we communicate with each other and the way we see reality. Lucid Dream Studio is a team of VR|AR|MR creative content specialist, researchers, technologists, marketing strategists, filmmakers, designers, and developers.
    Lucid Dream Studio is one of the first VR|AR|MR ‘Creative Studio’ in India and also one of the first to offer stereoscopic (3D) 360 Cinematic VR solutions. Founded by a creative producer and a content designer, LDS has the in-house capabilities of creative content creation and cinematic VR production. LDS has partnered with some of the best tech start-ups as well as established players in the industry to provide cutting-edge end-to- end VR|AR|MR solutions under one roof.

  • Hardware Solutions

    Vuze Camera - LDS is the first company in India to provide 3D 360 cinematic VR solutions. The Vuze camera, allows us to make the creation of true VR content accessible to just about anyone. Vuze camera is the world’s first high quality, high resolution 3D 360 virtual reality camera
    Insta360 Pro – LDS is the first company in India to procure the world’s first pro level 3D 360 camera. The Insta360 Pro captures stills and videos in stunning 8K with optical flow image stitching. It has inbuilt real-time image stitching and preview, so that you can live stream in 3D 360 as the story unfolds.
    Head Mounted Displays – LDS has a large selection of 2D and 3D head mounted displays for interactive and non-interactive VR. HMD’s like Samsung Gear VR, Google Cardboard enabled headsets, HTC Vive, Oculus Rift Touch, etc.
    Microsoft Hololens – Microsoft HoloLens is the first self-contained, holographic computer, enabling you to engage with your digital content and interact with holograms in the world around you. LDS provides customized solutions for Microsoft Hololens
    Customized Holographic Displays – LDS provides customized solutions for Holographic Displays using technologies like Laser, Electroholography, Full Parallax, etc.
